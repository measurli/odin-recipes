Description

This classic spinach lasagna features three different kinds of cheese. By adding the spinach, mushrooms, onions and pasta sauce, it'll have enough nutritious value.

Several reviewers have rated this high. They also recommended this for families with children.

Ingredients

  • 15 lasagna noodles
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ...

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna sheets until al dente.
  2. In a skillet, cook the diced mushrooms, onions and garlic.
  3. Cook the spinach for 5 minutes and drain. Squeeze out any excess water.
  4. Combine ricotta cheese, Romano cheese, egg, salt, oregano, basil, and pepper in a bowl.
  5. Add cooled mushroom mixture and spinach; beat with an electric mixer on low speed for 1 minute.
  6. In a baking dish, make layers of lasagna sheets, mushroom-spinach mixture, mozzarella, parmesan and pasta sauce.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour. Cool 15 minutes before serving.
