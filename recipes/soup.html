Chicken noodle soup

A quick and comformting soup, for sick days or whenever you need a pick-me-up.

Description

The hearty egg noodles add substance to a nourishing combination of vegetable and chicken broths, topped with savory spices like oregano and basil. Preparation and cooking take 40 minutes or less

Several reviewers have rated this high. They also recommended this for families with children.

Ingredients

  • broth
  • chicken breast
  • ...

Steps

  1. Cook onions and celery in oil until tender.
  2. Add chicken broth, vegetable broth, chicken, egg noodles, carrots, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
